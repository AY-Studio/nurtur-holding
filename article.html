SM Rentals will soon become Nurtur

January 15, 2025 London, UK

We're thrilled to announce an exciting new chapter in our journey. SM Rentals will soon be rebranding as Nurtur, marking a significant evolution in our mission to provide exceptional clinical seating solutions for healthcare providers across the UK.

Why Nurtur?

The name Nurtur reflects our core commitment to nurturing patient wellbeing through innovative seating solutions. It embodies everything we stand for: care, comfort, and the continuous support of both patients and healthcare professionals.

Our new brand identity represents more than just a name change. It's a reflection of our dedication to:

  • Enhancing patient comfort and dignity in healthcare settings
  • Supporting healthcare professionals with reliable, clinically-approved equipment
  • Fostering long-term partnerships with nursing homes, hospitals, and home care providers
  • Innovating continuously to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare sector

What's Changing?

While our name and visual identity are evolving, our commitment to excellence remains unchanged. You can expect:

The same exceptional service: Our dedicated team continues to provide the responsive, personalized support you've come to rely on.

The same quality products: All our clinical seating solutions remain designed by occupational therapists and clinically approved for use in healthcare settings.

The same rapid delivery: We'll continue to deliver chairs in less than 4 hours when you need them most.

Our Vision for the Future

As Nurtur, we're expanding our vision to become the UK's most trusted partner in clinical seating solutions. Our goals include:

  1. Expanding our product range: Introducing new seating solutions to meet diverse patient needs
  2. Enhanced digital experience: Making it easier than ever to book, manage, and track your equipment
  3. Strengthened partnerships: Building deeper relationships with healthcare providers across the country
  4. Sustainability initiatives: Implementing eco-friendly practices throughout our operations

What This Means for You

If you're a current customer, you don't need to do anything. All existing contracts, bookings, and service agreements will continue seamlessly under the Nurtur brand. Your account information, contact details, and service history remain exactly the same.

You'll begin to see our new branding appear across:

  • Our website and digital platforms
  • Equipment and delivery vehicles
  • Documentation and communications
  • Staff uniforms and identification
